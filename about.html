Our Story

More than a guide — your Taiwan buddy

Nxgo was born from a simple idea: Taiwan is too good to explore alone, and too beautiful to explore carelessly.

Why we built Nxgo

Taiwan packs night markets, mountain trails, hot springs, and coastal roads into an island you can cross in a day — yet most travelers still end up at the same five spots.

Nxgo connects you with local buddies, eco-volunteer crews, and a community of travelers who share your vibe. We believe every stop should count — for you and for the places you visit.

From tree-planting in Taichung to lantern walks in Jiufen, we're building a travel platform where adventure meets real impact.

Today, thousands of travelers use Nxgo to plan adventures, share stories, and make every journey through Taiwan unforgettable.
